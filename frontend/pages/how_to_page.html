A Tutorial To TETRIS

Use arrow keys left and right to move the blocks horizontally.

Arrow key up rotates the block.

Arrow key down increases the speed of the block falling.

Pressng the Space Bar allows you to 'hard drop' meaning the block will immediately reach the bottom.

If you stack the blocks too high the game is over!!

As the lines are cleared , the levels increase and so does the speed of the falling blocks .

Clearing multiple rows at once increases your scoring opportunities.

Score as many points as possible by clearing every square within a row!!!
