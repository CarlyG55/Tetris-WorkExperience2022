A Tutorial To TETRIS

In TETRIS you can either use your keyboard OR your mouse to play.

KEYBOARD

Use arrow keys left and right to move the blocks horizontally.

Arrow key up rotates the block to the right.

Pressing Z can rotate the block to the left.

Arrow key down increases the speed of the block falling.(This is called a Soft Drop)

By pressing the Space Bar the block will immediately reach the bottom.(This is called a Hard Drop)

By Pressing C you can hold the block.

By pressing the Esc Key you can pause your game

MOUSE

You can move the block right or left with the help of your mouse moving horizontally.

Right Mouse button will hold the block in place.

Left Mouse button will cause a hard drop (Immediately reaches the bottom)

(We recommend using a mouse since it's easier and more fast-paced compared to using a keyboard.)

Main Rules and SCORING

As the rows are cleared , the levels increase and so does the speed of the falling blocks .

Clearing multiple rows at once increases your scoring opportunities.

If you stack the blocks too high the game is over!!

Score as many points as possible by clearing every square within a row!!!
